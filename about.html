About Quiz Platform

Welcome to the Quiz Platform, a cutting-edge educational tool designed to enhance your knowledge across a wide range of computer science topics! Built with Firebase for secure authentication and Firestore for storing quiz results, this platform offers an interactive and personalized learning experience.

Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a professional sharpening your skills, or a curious learner exploring new subjects, our platform provides quizzes on topics like Operating Systems, DBMS, Computer Networks, and more. Each quiz is timed (20 minutes) and features a variety of questions to test your understanding, with immediate feedback to help you improve.

What sets us apart? Your progress is uniquely yours—results are saved per user, so you can track your performance over time. With an intuitive interface, vibrant design, and seamless navigation, learning becomes both fun and effective. Dive in to challenge yourself, review your results, and master key concepts at your own pace!
